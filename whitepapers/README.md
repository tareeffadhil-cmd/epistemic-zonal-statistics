EZS Whitepapers

This section hosts conceptual and methodological whitepapers related to the Epistemic Zonal Statistics (EZS) initiative.
The works below develop, extend, or critically examine position-aware and certainty-aware inference frameworks across statistics, diagnostics, epidemiology, education, finance, and cybersecurity.
